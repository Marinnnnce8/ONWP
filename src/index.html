Protecting Orkney Native Wildlife

Get Involved

The Orkney Native Wildlife Project aims to safeguard the unique and internationally important native wildlife of Orkney by addressing the threat it faces from an invasive non-native predator: the stoat.

Find out more about the project

The Orkney Native Wildlife Project is a partnership between RSPB Scotland, Scottish Natural Heritage and Orkney Islands Council with generous funding from the National Lottery and the EU's LIFE Programme.
